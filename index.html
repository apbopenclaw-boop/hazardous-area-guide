§ 02 — Standards quick reference

The standards behind every certification.

ATEX

Directive 2014/34/EU

EU directive governing equipment for explosive atmospheres. Mandatory CE marking. Requires Notified Body EU-Type Examination Certificate.

Scope: EU/EEA + Switzerland, Turkey
IECEx

IEC 60079 series

International certification scheme for explosive atmospheres. Accepted in 30+ countries including UK, Australia, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Brazil, and Singapore.

Scope: Global (voluntary)
1999/92/EC

Worker Protection

Requires employers to classify zones, select appropriate equipment, and implement an Explosion Protection Document. Transposed as DSEAR (UK), VEXAT (AT).

Key: Employer is responsible for correct equipment selection
